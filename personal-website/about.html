"I just wanted to create a website and community that can bring people together and put a smile on their face." ~Troy, "Icy TJ", Murphy Jr.

The ICY KICKS website was founded in 2020 by the world-famous, Troy Murphy Jr, a.k.a. Icy TJ. He always had a vision of bringing the sneaker community together and expand into a worldwide culture. He believes that sneakers can unite people of all colors, genders and cultures. The website shows sneakers that will be releasing soon and where to get them. Also, we offer a look at current trends and celebrities wearing shoes so that customers have a better idea of the shoe. Have fun scrolling everyone!
