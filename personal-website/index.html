Air Jordan 3 "Blue Cement"

Releases October 10, 2020

The Air Jordan 3 Varsity Royal comes with a Varsity Royal tumbled leather upper with elephant print overlays. Adding some contrast to the shoe is the Black detailing found on parts of the shoe such as the laces, heel tab and part of the midsole. White accents are also found on the rest of the midsole and the Jumpman branding on the heel. Cement Grey takes care of the lining, eyestay, tongue, most of the outsole and collar.

Air Jordan 4 "Paris Saint-Germain"

Releases October 10, 2020

The new colorway of the Air Jordan 4 flaunts a color scheme of White, Neutral Grey, Black, and Bordeaux — with the majority draped in White. Bordeaux accents and black detailing throughout comprise its aesthetic while finishing touches consist of the “PANAME” text label on the lateral forefoot and the “PSG” badge on its heels.

Union x Air Jordan 4 "Off Noir"

Releases October 16, 2020

This collaborative Air Jordan 4 in conjunction with Union features a mesh and nubuck construction on the upper with dark navy dominating most of the shoe and a royal blue on the ankle/heel area. White and red waffle eyelets, white mesh paneling on the tongue and side panels, translucent wings, a black heel tab with red Nike Air branding, and “AIR JORDAN” branding on the outer tongue which is traditionally seen on the inner tongue finish off the main details. An off-white/Sail midsole and outsole, a Union x Jumpman hang tag, and a special box with Union branding and Michael Jordan’s hands finish things off.

Adidas Yeezy Boost 350 V2 "Natural"

Releases October 24, 2020

Adidas and Kanye West will be debuting the adidas Yeezy Boost 350 V2 Natural this Fall season. Done in an official color scheme listed as Abez/Abez-Abez, this latest colorway of the model comes dressed in a Light Tin shade that matches its “Abez” name with Grey and White treads across the Primeknit upper. Other details on the shoe include a translucent stripe on the lateral side and a Cream-colored Boost midsole.

Air Jordan 1 High OG "Dark Mocha"

Releases October 31, 2020

This new Jordan 1 colorway starts off with a soft Sail leather construction that hits the toe, side panels, and ankle which is then contrasted by Dark Mocha suede overlays on the ankle/heel and Black leather overlays on the eyestay, midfoot, and toe. A black tongue tag with Sail Nike Air branding, black inner liner, black Wings logo, black Swooshes, Sail tongue with matching laces, a Sail rubber midsole, and a black rubber outsole finish off the look on this Air Jordan 1.
